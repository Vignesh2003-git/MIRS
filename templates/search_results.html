Summary:

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is a field of computing that focuses on developing machines that can perceive, understand, and react to their environment. AI systems use algorithms and data to simulate human intelligence, enabling computers to perform tasks that typically require human cognition, such as learning, problem-solving, and decision-making.
